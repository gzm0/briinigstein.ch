RENTAL

Rental agreements are made directly with the owner. Please send an e-mail message or phone for information. The chalet is only rented by the week, usually from Saturday to Saturday.

CONTRACT

The owner issues a rental contract. The reservation is considered final once the contract is signed by both contracting parties and a deposit of CHF 1000 is paid.
