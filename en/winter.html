SKIING (ALPINE, CROSS-COUNTRY, SNOWBOARDING)

4 cable cars, 4 chair lifts, 5 ski-lifts, 6 training lifts for children. snow-sure in winter, partially with artificial snow-making, fun park, carving slope, primed slopes in all levels of difficulty, cross-country ski run. Swiss Ski and Snowboard School for children and adults.

SLEDGING

Primed sledging track from Mägisalp to Bidmi. During good conditions also from Mossbielen to Reuti.

WINTER HIKING

Diverse winter hiking trails in the skiing region (marked in green on the map above), partially suited for baby strollers.

INDOOR SWIMMING POOL, CLIMBING HALL, MUSEUM

Meiringen offers a wide range of options and is easily reached on foot or by cable car.
